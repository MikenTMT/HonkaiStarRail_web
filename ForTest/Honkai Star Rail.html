About Honkai: Star Rail

Honkai: Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based RPG developed by miHoYo, renowned for its high-quality games in this genre. Set in a richly crafted sci-fi universe, players embark on thrilling adventures filled with excitement and intrigue. As they explore various planets, each location offers stunning landscapes, intricate storylines, and secrets waiting to be uncovered.
